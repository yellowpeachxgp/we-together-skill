# 触发词矩阵

## 强触发

- `we-together 模拟`
- `we-together what-if`
- `we-together simulate_year`
- `we-together simulate_week`
- `we-together dream_cycle`
- `we-together tick`

## 次触发

- `what-if`
- `simulate`
- `dream cycle`
- `projection`
- `rollback tick`

仅在已明确指向 `we-together` 时才生效。

## 非触发

- `当前状态`
- `交接文档`
- `图谱摘要`
- 泛化预测学 / 商业 forecasting 讨论
